About us..

The Aayozak Foundation is a non-governmental organisation (NGO) dedicated to fostering social awareness and community development across the Delhi NCR region. Established with a vision to create an informed and engaged society, the foundation leverages its extensive network of volunteers and community members to implement various social initiatives. Through strategic outreach and innovative programs, Aayozak Foundation aims to address pressing social issues and empower individuals to contribute positively to their communities.
